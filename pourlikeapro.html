Pour Like a Pro

When we think of a cookbook, the first image that usually comes to mind is a vibrant collection of greens and reds, accompanied by perfectly styled, mouthwatering food images. But I wanted to break away from this conventional approach and create something different. This cookbook isn’t about food—it’s about drinks. Specifically, alcoholic drinks. Designed for beginners, it’s a guide for those who often default to serving neat alcohol simply because they don’t know what else to make or how to pair drinks with the right occasion. The recipes are thoughtfully divided by occasions, making it easy to find the perfect drink for any event. Whether it’s a casual gathering, a festive celebration, or a special toast, this cookbook ensures you’ll never run out of ideas to impress your guests.
