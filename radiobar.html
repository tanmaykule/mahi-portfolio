Radio Bar

Radio Bar is a celebration of boldness, music, and opulence—a hotspot that thrives on its maximalist
charm. For this project, I had the pleasure of crafting an entire series of posts and stories for one of
their vibrant musical nights. Each design was a symphony of vivid colors, layered textures, and
dramatic typography that perfectly captured the bar’s lively spirit.
The stories and posts were designed to be as extravagant as the nights themselves—think bursts
of boldness, intricate patterns, and playful, energetic elements that channel the brand’s unapologetically
maximalist vibe. From bold graphics to intricate details, the visuals were crafted to immerse the
audience in the high-energy atmosphere of the bar, ensuring every design was an experience in itself.
This project was an absolute joy to work on—translating Radio Bar’s dynamic energy and lavish
aesthetics into visuals that resonate with its vibrant clientele. These are some of my favorites from
the series, showcasing my love for creating designs that are loud, bold, and unapologetically alive!
