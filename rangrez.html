Rangrez Pigments

Rangrez is a celebration of nature’s palette—a playful, vibrant brand that creates organic pigments
derived from real flowers. With each color, Rangrez invites you into a world where artistry
and sustainability blend seamlessly. The visual narrative for Rangrez is centered around a
chearfull explosion of color. The design embraces a cheerful yet sophisticated palette, inspired
by blooming marigolds, delicate hibiscus, and radiant bougainvillea. Soft, perfect circles and
brushstrokes symbolize the organic essence of flower-derived pigments, while playful, chearfull
patterns evoke creativity and spontaneity.
Typography is bold yet approachable, with circles and raw lines that mimic the softness of flowers
and rawness of kids art .The tagline, “make the right choice today,” that like a walking call for
the parents. Rangrez isn’t just a pigment company; it’s a movement toward more sustainable and
meaningful art-making. Whether it’s a canvas, a mural, or a simple craft project, Rangrez colors
transform every surface into a celebration of life and nature.
