Graden Glow Sunset

Garden Glow Sunsets is a collection of promotional print and digital media
posts and flyers designed for Hitansh Kulkarni, the founder and lead artist
of his band.He curates events under the titles Garden Glow Sunsets and
Electrosura.These deliverables were created as part of my freelance design
collaboration with In designing the promotional materials for Garden Glow
Sunsets,I focused on visually representing the fusion of surreal, electric,
and classical music that defines the event. To achieve this,
I incorporated elements like blooming flowers and tigers to symbolize
natural beauty and strength, while swirling clouds and abstract hands
evoke a surreal, dreamlike quality.
The vibrant color palette and bold typography were chosen to mirror
the dynamic energy of electric music, while the organic shapes
and classical-inspired motifs create a sense of balance and harmony.
By blending these contrasting elements, I aimed to capture the essence
of the music's fusion and translate it into a visual language that resonates
with the audience, immersing them in the event's unique vibe even before
they attend.
