Daily Grind

Daily Grind is a vibrant and fun coffee brand offering four unique flavors that bring energy to your
daily routine. Collaborating with this brand was an exciting and creative experience.
For this project, I designed a three-panel promotional Instagram post showcasing the playful and
colorful personality of the brand. The design format aligns with their ethos of making coffee an
enjoyable and dynamic part of life.
The deliverables include a cohesive set of posts that blend bold typography, cheerful illustrations,
and a lively color palette. Each panel emphasizes one of the four flavors, capturing the essence of the
brand’s energetic In creating these visuals, I focused on bringing out the fun and approachable character of Daily
Grind. I used vibrant hues and quirky graphics to reflect the brand’s youthful energy. Every element,
from the placement of the flavor icons to the text arrangement, was thoughtfully designed to
engage their audience and leave a lasting impression.
